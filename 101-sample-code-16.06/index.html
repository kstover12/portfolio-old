Hi, I'm a Product Manager that loves clean, user-focused design and development.

My path to the tech world was far from typical...

I played Volleyball and studied Economics at Santa Clara University (go broncos!). After graduating I worked in Sales/Marketing before transitioning to running a tech startup. Once I entered the startup world, I couldn't get enough and decided to make a career out of it, so I trained as a Full Stack Web Developer with a focus on UI/UX design. And the rest is history!

I grew up in Northern California and now live in beautiful Western Colorado. When I'm not pushing pixels and coding you can find me outside horseback-riding, paddling, climbing, hiking, camping, etc.

As a Product Manager, I plan, build and launch responsive web applications.
